The dream of a citizen on planet Earth

Posted by Mo Z3rban on Nov 2, 2014

The dream of a citizen on planet Earth

The world we live on today is definitely not what many of us deserve. Illogical explanations tend to be the trend. People don’t care about one another but care about numbers written on paper. We live in a world were everyone has the same dream and looks up to the same people. It’s as if we live in a factory that produces identical puppets. That is the world I live on today. Minds are no longer of value and hearts tend to be a tool to weaken some humans. People need to not be followers but they need to have a dream. We all should have a dream. It doesn’t matter how small or big your dream is but it’s important for you to dream. What is a humans’ life worth if there isn’t a dream to achieve and accomplish? Earth isn’t the best place for dreamers but dreamers are the only people that can change that. I have a dream myself. My dream is to make people happier. My dream is to make peoples’ life easier. My dream is to make this world a better place and humans use their minds. My dream is to live on a better earth than the one we live in today. This world makes it so hard for me to achieve my dream. Every few days a hundreds of reasons show up to make me quite on my dream. I am sure everyone with a dream goes through the same situation. The reasons are usually valid. It must be much easier to just live like another puppet. That’s why billions of people are living as puppets. But I am no puppet. Regardless whatever happens, whenever I close my eyes I see the world I want to live in. That’s why I will not quite on my dream because one day I won’t need to close my eyes to see it.
